Global Map Overall

The map showing PM2.5 for countries from 2010-2017. Darker color indicates higher PM2.5 while lighter color indicates lower PM2.5. The dark grey color indicates countries with no data recoded. According to the graph, it is easy to find that India, Saudi Arabia and Niger has highest PM2.5 from 2010 to 2017.

Average PM 2.5 in Continents

The line chart showing average PM2.5 for continent from 2010 to 2017. According to the graph, Asia has highest average PM2.5 from 2010 to 2015. Africa has highest PM2.5 from 2015 to 2017. The Oceania has lowest PM2.5 from 2010 to 2017. One interesting found in this graph is that North America, South America, Europe and Oceania has similary trend of changes in average PM2.5.
